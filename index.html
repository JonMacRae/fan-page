The Experience of Golf

The game of golf is as polorizing as any! Golf provides some with a relaxig pass time while driving others to act out in fits of rage. It is that polorizationthat makes the experience of golf such a fantastic topic. We will explore the good and the bad in more detail as we go.

The good

Golf has the ability to bring the worste out in all of us, but if you can resiste being one of those guys, you will learn to truely experience the joys that the game has to offer. Let's take a look at the subtle feelings that golf can provide.

The long ball:
This is the Quintessential imige of a domminent golfer, imperessing your friends and intimidating your opponents!
The well struck Iron:
The mark of an advanced golfer. These shots may not be as impressive as the 300 yard drive, but they do more for your score card and in the end they don't ask how you did it. The feeling of hitting a green from 200 yards is second to none.
The 20 foot putt:
While this is clearly the most dule of of the three to watch, Putting does more for your score card than any other shot. The mature golfer knows that these putts mean dollars in their pockets!
